Options for Netflix Rewind 1 sec

Use < or , to rewind with 1 second
Use > or . to skip with 5 seconds (does get stuck sometimes)

You can change the amount of seconds to rewind or seek forward.
Note: the default value on Netflix is 10 seconds.
